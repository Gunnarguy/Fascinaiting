Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use OpenAssistant?

OpenAssistant is designed to be intuitive and user-friendly. Simply open the app and start typing your question or request. The AI assistant will respond quickly with helpful information.

For detailed instructions, refer to the App Store description.

What should I do if the app crashes?

If you experience any crashes:

  1. Close the app completely and restart it
  2. Make sure your iOS device is updated to the latest version
  3. Check if an app update is available
  4. If the issue persists, please contact support with details about when the crash occurred

How can I provide feedback?

We appreciate your feedback to help improve OpenAssistant! You can:

  • Email us at Gunnarguy@me.com
  • Leave a review on the App Store
