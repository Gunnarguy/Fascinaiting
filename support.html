FAQs

How do I use OpenAssistant?

OpenAssistant is designed to help you with various tasks. Simply open the app and follow the on-screen instructions.

What should I do if the app crashes?

If the app crashes, please try restarting it. If the problem persists, contact our support team with details of the issue.

We value your feedback! Please send any comments or suggestions to feedback@openassistant.app.
